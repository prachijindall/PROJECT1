Skiing

Zermatt: Home to the iconic Matterhorn, Zermatt offers year-round skiing with some of the highest slopes in the Alps. The resort features over 360 km of pistes, suitable for all skill levels.

Paragliding

Interlaken: The adventure capital of Switzerland, Interlaken offers stunning flights over the lakes of Thun and Brienz, with views of the Jungfrau region’s mountains.

Mountain Biking

Graubünden: Offers over 4,000 km of marked trails, including the famous Alps Epic Trail from Davos to Arosa. It’s a mountain biking paradise with trails for all levels.
