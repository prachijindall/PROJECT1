Switzerland

Skiing

Zermatt: Home to the iconic Matterhorn, Zermatt offers year-round skiing with some of the highest slopes in the Alps. The resort features over 360 km of pistes, suitable for all skill levels.

Paragliding

Interlaken: The adventure capital of Switzerland, Interlaken offers stunning flights over the lakes of Thun and Brienz, with views of the Jungfrau region’s mountains.

Mountain Biking

Graubünden: Offers over 4,000 km of marked trails, including the famous Alps Epic Trail from Davos to Arosa. It’s a mountain biking paradise with trails for all levels.

London

Indoor Skydiving

Experience the sensation of freefall in a safe and controlled environment with indoor skydiving. It’s a great way to get a taste of skydiving.

Zip Now London

Experience the thrill of urban ziplining on one of the world's longest and fastest zip lines, with stunning views of the London skyline.

ArcelorMittal Orbit

Slide down the world’s longest and tallest tunnel slide or challenge yourself with an abseil down the 178-meter structure, offering an adrenaline rush with a view.

India

Rock Climbing

Hampi is not only a UNESCO World Heritage site but also a paradise for rock climbers.

Scuba Diving

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands offer some of the best scuba diving spots in India, with crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs

Trekking

India offers some of the world’s most spectacular trekking routes. Popular treks include the Roopkund Trek in Uttarakhand.

USA

White-Water Rafting

Experience the thrill of navigating the rapids of the Colorado River, which cuts through the Grand Canyon.

Kayaking

Explore the unique ecosystem of the Everglades by kayaking through its mangrove tunnels and open waters.

Hiking

One of the world’s most iconic natural wonders, the Grand Canyon offers a range of hiking trails that cater to all levels.
